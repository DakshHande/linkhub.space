Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is LinkHub?
A: LinkHub is an online portal that provides easy access to popular websites and resources. It is designed to simplify navigation across various websites and services by offering a single platform where you can quickly find and access your favorite online destinations.
Q: Is LinkHub a project or a commercial platform?
A: LinkHub is not a student project; it is a fully functional online platform. The site is maintained as an operational service with consistent content updates and features to make it useful for a wide audience.
Q: How does LinkHub generate revenue?
A: LinkHub is supported through online advertisements. These ads help maintain the platform and allow us to provide it as a free service to users. We do not require any payment for accessing our features, and ads are our primary source of revenue.
Q: Why do you restrict certain searches?
A: To maintain a safe and user-friendly environment, certain words and inappropriate content are restricted in the search feature. If banned words or links are detected, the search bar will be disabled for that user, and further access will require manual navigation through our provided links.
Q: How does the search ban work?
A: If any user tries to search for banned words or inappropriate links, their search bar will immediately disappear, and they will be restricted from using it even after refreshing the page. Instead, users are prompted to use the 'Manual Link' page to access our content. This restriction is managed using local storage in the browser, so the ban applies only to the specific browser and device used.
Q: What kind of searches are allowed on LinkHub?
A: We encourage users to search for popular and useful websites related to education, social media, shopping, entertainment, and news. Please avoid any language or content that is inappropriate, vulgar, or includes direct links or URLs, as these will trigger the ban feature.
Q: Why are certain topics restricted from being discussed?
A: LinkHub is designed to be a general-purpose resource platform, and as such, we prioritize safe and respectful use. Topics that could disrupt this environment or that involve sensitive, controversial, or harmful content are restricted.
Q: Can I request new websites or features?
A: While we welcome suggestions, we may not be able to fulfill all requests due to the site's purpose and design. Please reach out through the Contact page for any inquiries or feedback.
Q: What is the main concept behind LinkHub?
A: The main concept behind LinkHub is convenience and accessibility. Our platform aims to be a quick-access hub where users can find and reach their favorite websites without needing to type URLs directly. We focus on ease of use, clear navigation, and reliable performance.
Q: Can I ask questions about the development or coding of LinkHub?
A: Our development and coding practices are not open for discussion or disclosure. LinkHub is a fully developed site, and we do not provide information about the technical aspects or backend processes involved.

If you have further questions, please visit our Contact page.
